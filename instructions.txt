Building 

To build the project use following command:
mvn exec:java

Application works on http://localhost:8080/

*http://localhost:8080/api/book/{ISBN}
*http://localhost:8080/api/rating

Deployed on heroku server:
*https://stark-garden-29339.herokuapp.com/api/book/all
*https://stark-garden-29339.herokuapp.com/api/book/{ISBN}
*https://stark-garden-29339.herokuapp.com/api/rating
